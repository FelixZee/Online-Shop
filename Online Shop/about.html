M21 Sport About Us


M21 SPORT: We are a renowned local sports brand specializing in the production and distribution of high-quality football jerseys, sportswear, and accessories.

As the official Sport Attires sponsor of the Myanmar National League, we proudly partner with top teams like Yadanarbon FC, Dagon Star United FC, Myawady FC, and Genesi Esport. Our commitment is to support athletes at every level with premium gear designed for performance and style. Furthermore, we try to empower the local sports community for greater developments.

M21 Sport Collections: We are a leading distributor of globally famous Football team jerseys, available for retail and wholesale. In addition to offering a wide range of soccer shoes, sports gear, and accessories, we also provide professional printing services. As your one-stop destination for sports team jerseys, we ensure top-quality products and services made to meet every team's needs.

Our Branches are located in Yangon (Sanchaung, North Dagon), Mandalay and Myitkyina.
